# Creating Budgets

This guide walks you through creating budgets to manage your spending.

## Overview

A budget is an allocation of money from a wallet for a specific purpose. When you create a budget:

- Money is transferred from your wallet to the budget
- Your wallet balance decreases
- Your budget balance is ready to spend

Think of it like moving cash from your wallet into labeled envelopes.

## Creating a Single Budget

1. Go to **Budgets** from the main menu
2. Tap **Create Budget**
3. Select the **wallet** to allocate from
4. Enter a **name** for the budget (e.g., "Groceries", "Entertainment")
5. Enter the **amount** to allocate
6. Tap **Create**

Your wallet balance decreases by the allocated amount, and the budget is ready to use.

## Creating Multiple Budgets at Once

KaGet lets you set up multiple budgets in one go—perfect for monthly planning.

1. Go to **Budgets** from the main menu
2. Tap **Create Budget**
3. Tap **Add Budget** to add each budget:
   - Select the **wallet** to allocate from
   - Enter name and amount
   - Add as many as you need
4. Review the **summary** (grouped by wallet):
   - Total allocation per wallet
   - Remaining balance per wallet after allocation
5. Tap **Create All**

> **Example:** Monthly budget planning from multiple wallets
>
> | Budget         | Wallet       | Amount |
> | -------------- | ------------ | ------ |
> | Groceries      | Bank Account | $400   |
> | Transportation | Bank Account | $200   |
> | Entertainment  | Cash         | $150   |
> | Dining Out     | Cash         | $100   |
>
> **Summary:**
>
> - Bank Account: −$600 (remaining: $1,400)
> - Cash: −$250 (remaining: $250)

## Allocation Rules

| Rule                         | Description                                             |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cannot exceed wallet balance | Total allocation per wallet must be ≤ available balance |
| Minimum amount               | Allocation must be greater than zero                    |
| Multiple wallets allowed     | Each budget can come from a different wallet            |

## What You'll See After Creation

Each budget displays:

- **Name** — What the budget is for
- **Balance** — How much is left to spend
- **Source wallet** — Where the money came from
- **Progress** — Percentage used (for spending budgets)

## Tips

- **Plan monthly** — Create budgets at the start of each month
- **Be realistic** — Base amounts on past spending patterns
- **Leave buffer** — Don't allocate 100% of your wallet; keep some flexible funds
- **Use clear names** — "Weekly Groceries" is better than "Food"
